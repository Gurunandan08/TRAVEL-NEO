CHINA

Day 1

Day 1: Beijing - **Morning:** Visit the iconic Forbidden City and Tiananmen Square. - **Lunch:** Enjoy authentic Peking duck at Quanjude Roast Duck Restaurant. - **Afternoon:** Explore the historic Hutongs (narrow alleys) by rickshaw. - **Dinner:** Indulge in traditional Beijing cuisine at Fangshan Restaurant. - **Hotel:** Stay at the luxurious Grand Hyatt Beijing.

Day 2

Day 2: Xi'an - **Morning:** Discover the Terracotta Army at the Mausoleum of the First Qin Emperor. - **Lunch:** Taste local specialties like Xi'an noodles and dumplings at De Fa Chang Dumpling Restaurant. - **Afternoon:** Visit the ancient City Wall and explore the Muslim Quarter. - **Dinner:** Savor authentic Shaanxi cuisine at Defachang Restaurant. - **Hotel:** Stay at the elegant Grand Mercure Xi'an on Renmin Square.

Day 3

Day 3: Shanghai - **Morning:** Marvel at the skyline from the Bund and take a walk through Yu Garden. - **Lunch:** Try Shanghai's famous soup dumplings at Din Tai Fung. - **Afternoon:** Explore the modern side of Shanghai in Pudong, including the Oriental Pearl Tower and Shanghai Tower. - **Dinner:** Enjoy a mix of Chinese and international flavors at Lost Heaven on the Bund. - **Hotel:** Stay at the stylish Jing An Shangri-La, West Shanghai.

Day 4

Day 4: Chengdu - **Morning:** Visit the Giant Panda Breeding Research Base to see adorable pandas. - **Lunch:** Try Sichuan hot pot at Huangcheng Laoma Restaurant. - **Afternoon:** Explore the historic Jinli Ancient Street. - **Dinner:** Experience authentic Sichuan cuisine at Chen Mapo Tofu Restaurant. - **Hotel:** Stay at the tranquil Ritz-Carlton, Chengdu.
